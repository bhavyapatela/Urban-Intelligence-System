📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Insights

🧾 Dataset Overview
Total records: 2208 entries (hourly data)
Time period: October to December
Features include:
Environmental: temperature, humidity
Pollution: PM10, PM2.5, NO₂, CO, AQI
Traffic: traffic_index
Time-based: hour, day, month, day_of_week, weekend flag
🧹 Data Quality
✅ No missing values across all columns
✅ Data is already clean and structured
✅ Feature engineering performed:
Extracted time-based features (hour, day, month)
Created is_weekend and day_type
Generated target variable: aqi_next (for prediction)

🌡️ Environmental Insights
Average temperature: ~20.7°C
Temperature range: 8.6°C to 34.6°C
Humidity varies widely (21% to ~99%), indicating seasonal/weather variation

👉 Insight: Weather conditions fluctuate significantly and can influence pollution levels.

🌫️ Air Pollution Insights
Average AQI: ~173 (Moderate to Unhealthy)
Maximum AQI: ~290 (Very Poor category)
PM2.5 and PM10 show high variability:
PM10 max: 456
PM2.5 max: 333

👉 Insight: Air quality frequently reaches unhealthy levels, making prediction highly relevant.
High spikes indicate pollution events or peak traffic periods.

🚗 Traffic Insights
Traffic Index range: 0.05 to 1.0
Average: ~0.43

👉 Insight: Traffic intensity varies significantly across time.
Likely contributor to pollution spikes (especially CO, NO₂).

⏰ Temporal Patterns
Data captured hourly → strong time-based patterns
Features like hour, day_of_week, and is_weekend are important

👉 Observations:
Pollution and traffic vary by:
Time of day (rush hours)
Weekdays vs weekends
Weekend indicator helps model behavioral differences
